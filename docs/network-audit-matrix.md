## Notes

- Upstream automation uses `remote/shared/RecommendedPreferences.sys.mjs` to enforce a stricter no-network environment for controlled browser runs. Veil should borrow that evidence where product-compatible.
- Security-sensitive services remain in scope, but Veil will not disable them without replacement and documentation.
- Initial measured Veil startup audit artifacts live under `artifacts/network-audit/after-20260314/`.
- Reduced clean-profile startup audit artifacts live under `artifacts/network-audit/after-20260315-mozdata/`.
- Subscribed-profile Push verification artifacts live under `artifacts/push-profiles/subscribed-profile-no-uaid/` and `artifacts/network-audit/after-20260314-push-subscribed/`.
- Real upstream Push regression artifacts live under `artifacts/push-mochitest/20260315/`.
- Browser-attributed endpoints should be taken from `lsof_remote_endpoints` first. Interface-wide packet capture on `any` also saw unrelated ambient traffic from other processes in the workspace.
